Submit a blood or medicine request to get help from our community

Request Guidelines

  • Emergency Cases: For critical situations, also call emergency services (911)
  • Contact Info: Provide accurate contact information for donors to reach you
  • Location: Be specific about your location to help nearby donors
  • Blood Requests: Include patient's blood group and any special requirements
  • Medicine Requests: Specify exact medicine name, dosage, and quantity needed
  • Response Time: Expect responses within hours for urgent requests
